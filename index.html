Game Instruction


  • Round starts with 3 energy both sides. The maximum energy that players must have is 10.

  • Click "Energy+" if the enemy gains an energy

  • Click "Energy-" if the enemy used an energy.

  • "Round+" increment a round. When you click it, it adds 2 additional enemy energy. This is because every start of a round, both side will gain 2 energy.

  • If you mistakenly clicked "Round+", just click "Round-" and it will decrement the round and subtract 2 enemy energy.

  • Click "Pass" if the enemy did not attack this round. It will increment a round and add 2 enemy energy.

  • If the match is over, just click "Reset" and the round will reset to 1 and energy to 3.
